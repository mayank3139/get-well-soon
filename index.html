Dear Person.

In the gentle ebb of moonlit night, Amidst the shadows, soft and light, I send my wishes, pure and bright, For your spirit to take flight. Though the winds may howl, and skies may weep, Your grace and strength, forever deep, Shall guide you through this fleeting sleep, And into dawn's embrace, you'll sweep. Rest now, dear friend, in nature's embrace, Let time's gentle hand your worries efface, For like the sun, with its warm embrace, Your wellness shall soon find its place. At the end Get well soon
